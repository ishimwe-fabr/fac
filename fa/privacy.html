Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

1. Introduction

VoltHub ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account on our website
  • Request a repair service
  • Fill out forms or surveys
  • Contact us via email or phone
  • Subscribe to our newsletter

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, address, and payment information.

Automatic Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Cookie and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling repair requests
  • Sending service updates and confirmations
  • Responding to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • Sending marketing and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Analyzing website usage and improving our services
  • Detecting and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password storage with hashing
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website and services (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Law enforcement when required by law or court order
  • Third parties with your explicit consent

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Track website analytics
  • Personalize content
  • Manage user sessions

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though this may limit some website functionality.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites before providing your information.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You can request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal obligations to retain certain information.

11. International Data Transfers

If your information is transferred to countries outside your country of residence, these countries may have different data protection laws. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website with the date of revision.

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
