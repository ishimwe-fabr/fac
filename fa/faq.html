General Questions

VoltHub provides comprehensive electronics repair and maintenance services including laptop repair, desktop computer maintenance, smartphone repairs, and general electronic device troubleshooting. We handle hardware and software issues with expert technicians.

You can request a repair by clicking the "Request Repair" button on our website, filling out the repair request form with details about your device and issue, and our team will contact you to schedule an appointment.

We typically respond to repair requests within 24 hours. For urgent issues, we offer expedited service. Most repairs can be completed within 3-7 business days depending on the complexity.

Pricing & Payment

Repair costs vary depending on the device type and issue complexity. We provide free diagnostic assessments to give you an accurate quote before any work begins. You're under no obligation to proceed with repairs.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. Payment is due upon completion of repairs unless other arrangements are made.
